Kenya Spring 2020

Kenya is home to some of the most iconic wildlife in the world, including the Big Five - lions, elephants, rhinos, leopards, and buffalo. Visitors to the country's national parks and game reserves can witness these majestic animals in their natural habitats, as well as other unique species such as giraffes, zebras, and cheetahs.

  • Explore kenya

Eritrea Fall 2021

Eritrea is a small country located on the Red Sea coast of Africa, offering visitors a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. One of its most popular tourist destinations is the ancient port city of Massawa, which features a beautiful beach that is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the views of the sea.

  • Explore Massawa

Ethiopia Summer 2022

Ethiopia is a country located in the Horn of Africa, and is home to a wealth of cultural and natural attractions that make it a popular tourist destination. One of the most vibrant and dynamic cities in the country is Addis Ababa, the capital city, which offers a mix of history, culture, and modern amenities.
